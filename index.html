ABOUT US

At Brew & Bite, our story began in 2020 with a simple dream — to create a cozy corner where people could pause, relax, and share moments over a perfect cup of coffee. What started as a small neighborhood café quickly grew into a beloved spot for coffee lovers, foodies, and friends seeking comfort in every sip and bite.
